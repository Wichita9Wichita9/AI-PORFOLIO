Professional Summary

I am a construction and real estate professional based in Omaha, Nebraska, with more than 20 years of experience across commercial real estate, acquisitions, due diligence, construction management, and field inspection. My background includes managing large-scale acquisitions and development projects, performing detailed site and property analysis, and supporting construction teams through field observation, documentation, quality assurance, and coordination.

Before that, I worked as an Acquisition and New Construction Manager for Blue Acres Group / OPAC Construction, helping lead underwriting and management of more than $125 million in acquisitions and development projects.

This portfolio reflects my interest in how AI can improve construction workflows, field review, code analysis, project management, and practical decision-making in the AEC industry.
