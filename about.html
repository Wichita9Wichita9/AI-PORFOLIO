Featured Project

Omaha Residential Construction Reviewer

A custom mini-agent designed to help a general contractor and owner’s representative review field-installed work, subcontractor scope, likely code issues, and next actions for ground-up single-family, duplex, triplex, and fourplex construction.

  • Reviews field photos against drawings and scope
  • Flags likely workmanship and coordination issues
  • Separates confirmed issues from likely issues needing verification
  • Supports construction-focused decision-making without replacing professional judgment
